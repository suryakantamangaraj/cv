# Contributing to Suryaraj CV

Thank you for your interest in helping maintain the quality of this project. As this is a personal portfolio and professional CV, contributions are limited to maintaining the accuracy and security of the content.

## 🌈 Scope of Contributions

I welcome contributions in the following three areas:

1.  **Security Issues:** If you find a security vulnerability, please refer to the [Security Policy](SECURITY.md) for instructions on how to report it safely.
2.  **Spelling & Grammar Mistakes:** If you spot a typo or grammatical error in the CV content or documentation.
3.  **Disputed Content:** If you notice any information that is incorrect, outdated, or potentially controversial.

## 🛠️ How to Contribute

For spelling mistakes or disputed content:
1.  **Open an Issue:** Describe the error or the disputed content clearly.
2.  **Pull Request:** If you have a fix, feel free to submit a small PR.
    -   Fork the repo.
    -   Create a branch for your fix.
    -   Submit the PR against the `dev` branch.

Please keep contributions focused strictly on these areas. For general feature requests or design changes, please open a discussion or reach out directly.

## 🤝 Community

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
